FAQ

Is solar effective in Wisconsin?

Yes, Wisconsin’s solar panels generate 1,200 kWh per kW annually, effective even in cloudy or cold conditions, thanks to modern panel technology.

How much solar do I need?

Average homes need 6–8 kW systems to offset 100% of their electricity use, depending on consumption.

Can I afford solar?

With a 30% federal tax credit, state rebates, and financing, solar costs have dropped 50% since 2015, making it accessible for many.

Quick Fact: Solar panels in Wisconsin have a payback period of 7–10 years with current incentives.
