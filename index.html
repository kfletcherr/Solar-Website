Solar Power in Wisconsin

Solar energy is becoming a bigger part of Wisconsin’s power system, with over 3,000 MW of capacity installed as of 2025. It offers some clear benefits, like reducing emissions and creating jobs, but it also comes with challenges, like weather limitations and high upfront costs. After researching and building this site, I think solar has potential, but it’s not a perfect solution. There are pros and cons, and it’s important to understand both when thinking about the future of energy in Wisconsin.
